Digital Literacy Bootcamp - Empowering Students with Digital skills incl...

By Peter July 2023

Through iCASE Africa in July 2023, I volunteered to empower students of ST. Stephen's public secondary school, Ile-Ife (Osun State) with crucial digital skills. Our goal was clear: equip these eager learners to navigate the digital world confidently. We focused on internet usage, Microsoft Word proficiency, and typing skills, witnessing their enthusiasm despite limited access to technology.

Guiding students through internet basics, Microsoft Word functionalities, and typing techniques was incredibly rewarding. Volunteering with iCASE Africa allowed me to enhance their academic prospects and instill a sense of empowerment. Departing, I felt hopeful for the bright futures of these resilient individuals, armed with essential digital skills for success.

“At the end of the day it’s not about what you have or even what you’ve accomplished… it’s about who you’ve lifted up, who you’ve made better. It’s about what you’ve given back.” by: Denzel Washington
